About Us

The XiangLands Survival Multiplayer was created on the 18th July 2020 during the pandemic, when a group of friends came together jokingly and created a Minecraft world to hang out, and that was the start of our journey and yet after 9 seasons and countless threats, the SMP still thrives today! We also run an official YouTube channel dedicated to the SMP where our members can participate in, as we have been planing to open a business firm and producing merchandise of our own and expanding our audience. Personally while writing this I would say it is a long journey ahead, and now, it depends on YOU if you take the red or the blue pill.
